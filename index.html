Hi, I am Brenna!

I am a PhD student in the Computer Science Department at the University of Toronto. I work in the intersection of Human Computer Interaction (HCI) and Health care with Professors Khai Truong and Alex Mariakakis.

My general research interests are in exploring how technologies can benefit Physicians and Patient interactions.
